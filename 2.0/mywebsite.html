What This Is All About

I'm learning how to code with HTML and CSS! I wrote all this code myself. No Wix, Wordpress, Squarespace, or any of those website design services. Cool, huh?


The idea is to use as many different HTML and CSS techniques as possible. Elements, attributes, whatever. Every time I learn a new skill, I incorporate it into this website. It's good practice. I'm also learning Photoshop, as my cover image indicates.

Where I Live

I live in Minneapolis, Minnesota.

The name Minneapolis is attributed to Charles Hoag, the city's first schoolmaster, who combined mni, a Dakota Sioux word for water, and polis, the Greek word for city.


In 2012, the Walker Art Center in Minneapolis hosted the world’s first Internet Cat Video Festival. Approximately 10,000 people attended.


Bob Dylan attended the University of Minnesota from 1959 to 1960, and was involved in the Minneapolis folk music scene. He dropped out after his freshman year to move to New York.
